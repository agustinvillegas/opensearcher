# prospect requirements

## location
enter the geographic area where businesses should be searched.
Examples:
- city
- region
- country
- neighborhood


## categories
enter the types of businesses to search for.

example format:
- category 1
- category 2
- category 3


## quantity
Enter the maximum number of businesses to collect.


## prefer
optional preferences that improve lead quality but are not mandatory.

examples:
- businesses without websites
- businesses with many reviews
- multiple locations
- active social media presence


## avoid
optional characteristics that should reduce priority or exclude results.

examples:
- franchises
- very small businesses
- businesses that already have a modern website

## (optional extra) target profile:
businesses that likely receive many customer inquiries and could benefit from a better digital presence.
