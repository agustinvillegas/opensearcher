# prospect requirements — España

## location
Madrid, Barcelona, Valencia, Sevilla, Málaga

## quantity
30

## prefer
- businesses without a website
- businesses with an outdated or low-quality website (PageSpeed mobile < 50)
- businesses with many reviews (> 30)
- single-location local businesses
- businesses that rank poorly on Google Maps

## avoid
- franchises or chains
- businesses with a modern, well-optimized website (PageSpeed > 80)
- corporate brands
- very small businesses with no review presence

## target profile
Local independent businesses in competitive service sectors (health, beauty, home services, legal, property) that rely on local search for customer acquisition. They likely have an existing web presence but it's outdated, slow, or non-existent — making them ideal candidates for a modern website that improves conversion and SEO.
